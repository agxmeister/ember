# Ember

A weight tracking app for Android that helps you observe how your weight changes over time.

## Onboarding

On first launch the app asks for your current weight and the hour your day typically starts. A daily reminder is scheduled 15 minutes after that time.

## Features

### Day Clusters
Your waking hours are split into equal time slots so that trends are built from comparable data — morning weigh-ins stay separate from evening ones. Each slot holds at most one measurement per day; re-checking in the same slot replaces the previous value.

### Weight Tracking
The home screen shows which cluster you are currently in and pre-fills the last recorded weight for quick check-ins.

### Charts
Per-cluster line charts display your weight trend over time, keeping data points comparable across days.

### Reminders
A daily notification reminds you to weigh in shortly after your configured wake time.
